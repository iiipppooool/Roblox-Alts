Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our alt testing services

How does alt account testing work? +
Our network of alternative accounts joins your Roblox game server to simulate real player behavior. This helps test server performance, identify bottlenecks, and optimize your game for better player experience.
Is this service safe and compliant with Roblox ToS? +
Yes, our testing services are designed to help developers optimize their games within Roblox's terms of service. We focus on legitimate performance testing and game improvement.
How quickly can I get results? +
Most tests begin within 24 hours of your request, with initial results available immediately during testing. Comprehensive reports are delivered within 48 hours.
Can I test multiple games with one package? +
Yes! All our packages include unlimited testing access, so you can test multiple games or run multiple test sessions on the same game.
What kind of reports do I receive? +
Reports include server performance metrics, player behavior analysis, bottleneck identification, optimization recommendations, and detailed analytics based on your package level.
